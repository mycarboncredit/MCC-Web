Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15-07-2026

My Carbon Credit respects user privacy and is committed to protecting information submitted through the Website. This policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and protected.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on user activity, the Website may collect:

  • Account and profile information voluntarily provided by users.
  • Lifestyle, mobility, energy, food, and sustainability responses used for carbon footprint estimation.
  • Technical information needed for secure operation, authentication, and basic usage analytics.

2. Purpose of Collection

Information is used only for carbon footprint calculation, personalized sustainability insights, academic research, environmental awareness, and anonymized statistical analysis.

3. Confidentiality

Individual responses will remain confidential and will not be shared with third parties except where required by law or with explicit user consent.

4. Aggregated Reporting

Research findings and environmental statistics may be reported only in aggregated and anonymized form, without identifying individual users.

5. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, withdrawal of consent, or deletion of their personal information through the contact details provided on the Website.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.
