About MyCarbonCredit

At MyCarbonCredit, we believe that sustainability begins with awareness and grows through action. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to understand, manage, and reduce their environmental impact through data-driven carbon footprint assessment and carbon offset solutions.

We are developing a digital platform that helps users measure their carbon footprint based on everyday activities such as transportation, home energy consumption, lifestyle choices, and workplace practices. By transforming complex environmental data into meaningful insights, we aim to make sustainability accessible, measurable, and actionable.

What We Do

Our platform enables users to:

  • Assess their carbon footprint through a simple and interactive questionnaire
  • Understand the key contributors to their emissions
  • Receive personalized recommendations for reducing their environmental impact
  • Explore carbon offset opportunities through afforestation, renewable energy, and verified carbon credit initiatives
  • Track sustainability progress over time

For organizations, the platform provides employee-level sustainability analytics that can support ESG reporting, sustainability initiatives, and carbon reduction strategies.

All information provided will be used solely for research and analytical purposes, and findings will be reported only in aggregated and anonymized form. Individual responses will remain confidential and will not be shared with third parties. 🌍
